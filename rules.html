RULES

The goal of this multi player dice game is to get the most points. There are 5 rounds of the game:

1- S
2- N
3- A
4- K
5- E

For each round, the current player rolls the dice.

If current player rolls any combination that doesn't include a 'snake eye' (a single pip on the die i.e. a die face with a value of one) they count their current points, and have the option to keep rolling, or end their turn ('Hold') and pass the dice to the next player.

If their roll includes a single snake eye, the player will collect ZERO points for that round and must pass the dice to the next player.

If their roll includes double snake eyes, the player will lose ALL accumulated points and must pass the dice to the next player.

The player with the most points on the 5th round wins.
